雙層泡沫溫度（0–100）：所有子訊號各自以「分段線性映射」把原始指標轉為 0（冷靜）–100（極端泡沫）分數，再按權重加權。市場層：巨頭資本支出 20%、估值熱度 20%、價格動能 15%、基礎設施溢價 15%、信用與投機 15%、市場集中度 15%。前瞻層：GPU 現貨租金 25%、供需剪刀差 20%、資料中心壓力事件 20%、循環交易 15%、徵才成熟度 10%、收入缺口 10%。映射節點寫死於程式碼，方法完全透明、可重現；分數高低代表「偏離歷史常態的程度」，並非漲跌預測。
前瞻層資料源：GPU 現貨租金 = vast.ai 公開市場 API（H100 SXM / H200 / A100 SXM4 單卡 on-demand 報價中位數），歷史錨點為公開報導約數；回本帶 $1.3–1.9/hr 為「採購 $25–30k、5 年折舊、含電力機房、70% 利用率」之全成本粗估。開發者採用 = npm 官方下載統計 API（openai 與 @anthropic-ai/sdk 套件，52 週週頻），需求年增率為兩套件平均；注意下載量含 CI/CD 流量，看趨勢不看絕對值。資料中心壓力事件 = Google News 中英文關鍵詞雷達，逐標題分類（取消/退租/延後 vs 動工/簽約/擴建）；設定 ANTHROPIC_API_KEY 時由 Claude 分類，否則為關鍵詞規則。循環交易 = 公開報導之大型「供應商投資客戶→客戶回購算力」結構台帳（承諾級別約數）+ 新增報導監測。徵才成熟度 = Hacker News「Who is hiring」月帖（Algolia API）關鍵詞分類。收入缺口 = 年化 CapEx×2（Sequoia 框架）對照公開報導之 AI 終端收入估計。
行情：Yahoo Finance（yfinance 批次抓取，日線一年，調整後收盤價）。漲跌幅口徑：1日 / 1週(5交易日) / 1月(21) / 6月(126) / 1年。美股慣例配色：綠漲紅跌。
資本支出：微軟、Alphabet、亞馬遜、Meta、甲骨文季度現金流量表（Yahoo Finance），CapEx 取絕對值；年增率為同季比較，合計年增率僅累計同時有今年/去年同季資料的公司。各家財年季度截止日不同，「最新季」以各自最近一季為準。
基本面：PE（TTM）、前瞻 PE、PS（TTM）、營收增速（最近季 YoY）、毛利率均來自 Yahoo Finance 彙總欄位，可能存在口徑差異，僅供橫向比較。
高收益債利差：設定 FRED_API_KEY 時抓取 ICE BofA US High Yield OAS（FRED：BAMLH0A0HYM2）；否則以 HYG−IEF 六個月相對表現作為方向代理。

歷史對照：定性整理（約數），供框架參考。

Twin bubble thermometers (0–100): each sub-signal maps its raw indicator to a 0 (calm) – 100 (extreme bubble) score via piecewise-linear mapping, then weights are applied. Market layer: hyperscaler capex 20%, valuation heat 20%, momentum 15%, infrastructure premium 15%, credit & speculation 15%, concentration 15%. Frontier layer: GPU spot rent 25%, supply–demand scissors 20%, DC stress events 20%, circularity 15%, hiring maturity 10%, revenue gap 10%. Mapping anchors are hard-coded — fully transparent and reproducible. Scores measure deviation from historical norms, not price forecasts.
Frontier sources: GPU spot rent = vast.ai public market API (median on-demand per-GPU asks for H100 SXM / H200 / A100 SXM4); historical anchors are public-report approximations; the $1.3–1.9/hr breakeven band is a full-cost estimate ($25–30k purchase, 5-yr depreciation, power & facility, 70% utilization). Developer adoption = official npm download API (openai and @anthropic-ai/sdk, 52 weekly points); demand YoY is the two-package average; downloads include CI/CD traffic, so watch the trend, not the level. DC stress = bilingual Google News keyword radar, each headline classified (cancel/lease-exit/delay vs groundbreaking/signing/expansion); Claude classifies when ANTHROPIC_API_KEY is set, otherwise keyword rules. Circularity = a ledger of large publicly reported "supplier invests in customer → customer buys compute back" structures (commitment-level approximations) plus new-report monitoring. Hiring maturity = Hacker News "Who is hiring" monthly threads (Algolia API), keyword-classified. Revenue gap = annualized capex ×2 (Sequoia framework) vs publicly reported AI end-revenue estimates.
Quotes: Yahoo Finance (yfinance batch, one year of daily adjusted closes). Change windows: 1D / 1W (5 sessions) / 1M (21) / 6M (126) / 1Y. US color convention: green up, red down.
Capex: quarterly cash-flow statements for Microsoft, Alphabet, Amazon, Meta and Oracle (Yahoo Finance), capex as absolute value; YoY compares the same fiscal quarter; the aggregate YoY only includes companies with both this-year and last-year data. Fiscal quarter-ends differ, so "latest quarter" is per company.
Fundamentals: PE (TTM), forward PE, PS (TTM), revenue growth (latest quarter YoY) and gross margin come from Yahoo Finance summary fields; definitions may differ — for cross-sectional comparison only.
High-yield spread: with FRED_API_KEY set, ICE BofA US High Yield OAS (FRED: BAMLH0A0HYM2); otherwise HYG−IEF 6-month relative performance as a directional proxy.
Executive brief: written by Claude from this snapshot's facts when ANTHROPIC_API_KEY is set (tagged AI-generated); otherwise a rules template. All numbers come from this page's snapshot.
Historical analogy: qualitative framing (approximate figures).
News radar: Google News (Traditional Chinese) keyword feeds; click through to the original story.
For research reference only — not investment advice.
