    METHODOLOGY & ASSUMPTIONS

    數據做法與假設

    L1 管制現況表 — 人工策展的官方規則表(BIS/荷蘭出口政策/中國商務部公告),逐條標 as_of 日期與證據等級(T1 官方規則、T2 權威報導、T3 新聞/推估)。「方向分數」= 近 12 個月各事件(收緊 +1 / 放鬆 −1)× 策展權重 × 線性遞減的時近權重(18 個月歸零),> +0.5 判 TIGHTENING、< −0.5 判 EASING。假設:方向只描述過去,政策天生不連續,不可外推。管制「水位」(結構性緊)與「方向」分開呈現,避免把邊際緩和誤讀成鬆綁。

    L2 第二鏈完整度 — 7 環節(EDA/設備/先進邏輯/HBM/先進封裝/互連/軟體)各給一個中國國產能力 %,全部是推估(EST)並標證據等級與 as_of;中國境內良率與成本無官方數據。綜合分數 = 0.7 × 最弱環節 + 0.3 × 平均(Liebig 最小值定律,方法與 /bottleneck 對齊);設備環節內部再取子項最小值(微影決定)。CoWoS 產能與西方鏈瓶頸數字見 /bottleneck 卡,不在本卡重寫。

    L3 即時層 — yfinance 中國半導體籃(中芯/華虹/北方華創/中微/寒武紀)對照西方設備籃(ASML/LRCX/AMAT)的 1 個月價格動能;明確標示為情緒代理,計分權重 0(與 /pricing 同一誠實規則)。Google News RSS 雙語新聞雷達,標題分類(升級/緩和/中性)為關鍵字規則,非模型判斷。抓不到的項目退回種子值並取消 live 標記。

    L4/L5 合成 — 刷新且有 ANTHROPIC_API_KEY 時由 Claude Opus 4.8 產出策略判讀與情境機率(結構化輸出);否則走確定性規則後備,頁首 engine 欄標注實際引擎。情境機率為判斷輔助。手動密碼刷新,無排程。非投資建議。

    L1 control-regime table — hand-curated official rules (BIS / Dutch export policy / MOFCOM), each row dated (as_of) and tier-graded (T1 official rule, T2 authoritative reporting, T3 news/estimate). The direction score = each trailing-12-month move (tighten +1 / ease −1) × curated weight × linear recency decay (zero at 18 months); > +0.5 reads TIGHTENING, < −0.5 EASING. Assumption: direction describes the past only — policy is discontinuous by nature and must not be extrapolated. The regime LEVEL (structurally tight) is shown separately from the direction so marginal easing is never misread as deregulation.

    L2 second-chain completeness — 7 links (EDA / equipment / advanced logic / HBM / advanced packaging / interconnect / software), each with a China-domestic capability %, ALL estimates (EST) with tier and as_of; no official yield/cost data exists inside China. Composite = 0.7 × weakest link + 0.3 × mean (Liebig's law of the minimum, method aligned with /bottleneck); within the equipment link the sub-component MIN governs (litho). CoWoS capacity and the western chain's own bottleneck numbers live on the /bottleneck card and are not re-written here.

    L3 live layer — yfinance China-semis basket (SMIC / Hua Hong / NAURA / AMEC / Cambricon) vs a western-tools contrast (ASML / LRCX / AMAT), 1-month price momentum; explicitly a sentiment PROXY with weight 0 in the score (same honesty rule as /pricing). Google News RSS bilingual radar; headline classification (escalation / de-escalation / neutral) is keyword-rule based, not model judgment. Any failed fetch falls back to seeds and loses its live badge.

    L4/L5 synthesis — Claude Opus 4.8 (structured output) on refresh when ANTHROPIC_API_KEY is set, deterministic rules fallback otherwise; the engine badge in the header shows which ran. Scenario probabilities are a judgment aid. Manual password-gated refresh only, no schedule. Not investment advice.
