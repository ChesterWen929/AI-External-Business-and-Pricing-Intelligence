      METHODOLOGY & ASSUMPTIONS

      五層架構 — L1 類比映射字典（每組配對「為何成立／哪裡失效」，人工策展，失效欄是本卡核心而非註腳）；L2 為 1995–2002 季度歷史序列（策展重建值，錨點間線性內插，逐序列標 T2/T3——歷史重建是近似，不是量測）；L3 為純規則最近鄰時鐘引擎；L4/L5 由 Claude 於刷新時合成（無 API key 走規則後備，頁首 engine 欄標注實際引擎）。

      今日輸入逐項來源 — capex 年增率：對齊 /aibubble（live yfinance 財報，T1）；GPU 現貨÷損益兩平：對齊 /aibubble vast.ai 掛牌與估計損益帶（T2）；循環融資比：/aibubble 循環台帳 ÷ TTM capex（T3，分子為累計多年期承諾、分母為一年——口徑不對稱，視為上界）；HY OAS：FRED BAMLH0A0HYM2（T1）；token 價格通縮、前十大權重、募資倍數：本卡推估（T3/EST，來源與時點逐項標注）。刷新時盡力讀取兄弟卡 snapshot 與 FRED；讀不到即退回種子值並取消 live 標記。

      時鐘怎麼算 — 每組配對把今日（水準、一年斜率）做 z 標準化後，在該歷史序列 1996Q1–2002Q4 找最近鄰 → 該組時鐘讀數＋距離信心（exp(−d)）。合成時鐘＝各讀數的加權中位數（權重＝策展映射權重 × 信心）；「±」為加權平均絕對離差。分歧度本身就是訊號：分歧越大＝兩個時代在結構上越不像。超出歷史區間的讀數以 ⚠ 標注——類比對該數值沒有座標。

      假設與界線 — 類比不是因果；歷史樣本 n=1；映射選擇本身是假設（逐組帶證據層級與權重）。時鐘是歷史座標，非預測——前瞻機率見 /scenario。手動密碼刷新（非排程）。非投資建議。

      Five-layer design — L1 is a hand-curated analogue-mapping dictionary (per pair: why the mapping holds / WHERE IT BREAKS — the breaks column is the core of this card, not a footnote). L2 is curated 1995–2002 quarterly history (reconstructions, linearly interpolated between anchors, tiered T2/T3 per series — reconstructions are approximations, not measurements). L3 is a rules-only nearest-neighbour clock engine; L4/L5 are synthesized by Claude on refresh (rules fallback without an API key; the engine badge shows which ran).

      Today's inputs, per item — capex YoY: aligned to /aibubble (live yfinance report data, T1); GPU spot ÷ breakeven: aligned to /aibubble vast.ai listings + estimated breakeven band (T2); circular-financing ratio: /aibubble circularity ledger ÷ TTM capex (T3 — cumulative multi-year commitments over a one-year denominator: definitional asymmetry, treat as upper bound); HY OAS: FRED BAMLH0A0HYM2 (T1); token-price deflation, top-10 weight, funding multiple: our estimates (T3/EST, each with source + as-of date). On refresh the card best-effort reads sibling snapshots + FRED; anything unreadable falls back to seeds and loses its live badge.

      How the clock is computed — each pair z-normalizes today's (level, 1-yr slope) and finds the nearest neighbour on its 1996Q1–2002Q4 curve → a per-pair reading + distance confidence (exp(−d)). Composite = weighted median of readings (weight = curated mapping weight × confidence); the "±" is the weighted mean absolute deviation. Dispersion is itself the signal: the wider it is, the more structurally unlike the two eras are. Beyond-range readings are flagged ⚠ — the analogue has no coordinate for that value.

      Assumptions & limits — analogy is not causality; n=1 historical sample; the mapping choices are themselves assumptions (tiered and weighted per pair). The clock is a historical coordinate, not a forecast — forward probabilities live at /scenario. Manual password-gated refresh only. Not investment advice.
