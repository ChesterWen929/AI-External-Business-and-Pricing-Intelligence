      METHODOLOGY & ASSUMPTIONS

      五層架構 — L1 玩家地圖(五類持有者與各自的解倉行為)與 L2 指標字典是人工策展知識庫(寫死於 KB,非即時);L3 是量化即時層;L4 誰先解倉·部位判讀·台積電視角與 L5 情境/證偽由 Claude 於刷新時合成(無 API key 走規則後備,頁首 engine 欄標注實際引擎)。

      L3 數據來源 — CFTC COT:傳統報告(僅期貨)Socrata 免 key API,那斯達克 100 e-mini 非商業淨部位,週二基準、週五發布;即時刷新用抓到的約三年歷史計算「真」百分位。NAAIM(週三)、AAII(週四)、CBOE 股票 put/call:免 key best-effort 抓取,失敗退回帶日期的策展種子。FINRA 融資餘額:官方月度數據但發布落後約兩個月(2026-07-02 能拿到的最新值是 2026-04-30 的 ~$1,190B)——刻意不做即時抓取,只用誠實標註時點的策展種子。yfinance 代理(標 PROXY,可靠即時):^VIX÷^VIX3M 期限結構、QQQ/SPY 20 日成交金額比、SOXX 相對量能。

      百分位與擁擠度貢獻 — 每個指標對照三年百分位:COT 在即時刷新時用真實歷史計算(標 live_3y),其餘用 KB 策展錨點線性內插(標 curated_band,屬估計值)。擁擠度貢獻 = 百分位;對「越低越擁擠」的指標(put/call、VIX 期限結構)取 100 − 百分位。

      綜合擁擠度(0=不擁擠,100=單邊) — 權重寫死並揭露:COT 20%、融資餘額年增 20%、NAAIM 15%、put/call 15%、AAII 10%、VIX 期限結構 10%、QQQ/SPY 量比 5%、SOXX 相對量 5%。結論門檻:<40 UNCROWDED、40–75 CROWDED、≥75 ONE-SIDED。細膩判讀(nuance):裂紋訊號 3 項——/credit 的 CCC−IG 解壓縮旗標(對齊 /credit 2026-07-02,ON)、VIX÷VIX3M ≥0.95、put/call ≥0.70 且上升;≥2 項亮 = 「擁擠且龜裂」,否則按部位趨勢(COT/NAAIM/融資餘額 4 週變化多數向上)分「擁擠且加碼」或「擁擠但停滯」。

      假設與限制 — COT 僅期貨、不含現貨與選擇權;AAII 是小樣本問卷;融資餘額落後兩個月;13F(季度+45 天)宣告不在免費層;策展區間上的百分位是估計;量能代理衡量注意力而非部位;0DTE 流無免費結構化來源。部位不是需求訊號——它決定回檔的猛烈度,不決定基本面。/aibubble(market 64.6 過熱,2026-07-02)僅作脈絡引用,不進入計分。手動密碼刷新,非排程。AI 合成內容為解讀輔助,非投資建議。

      Five-layer design — the L1 player map (five holder classes, each with its typical unwind behavior) and L2 indicator dictionary are a hand-curated knowledge base (static, not live). L3 is the quantitative live layer; L4 who-unwinds-first + positioning read + TSMC view and L5 scenarios/falsification are synthesized by Claude on each refresh (rules fallback without an API key; the engine badge shows which ran).

      L3 sources — CFTC COT: legacy report (futures only) via the keyless Socrata API, NASDAQ-100 e-mini net non-commercial, Tuesday as-of / Friday publish; live refresh computes the TRUE percentile from ~3 years of fetched history. NAAIM (Wed), AAII (Thu), CBOE equity put/call: keyless best-effort fetches falling back to dated curated seeds. FINRA margin debt: official monthly data published with a ~2-month lag (the freshest print available on 2026-07-02 is 2026-04-30, ~$1,190B) — deliberately NOT fetched live; the honestly-dated curated seed is the source of record. yfinance proxies (flagged PROXY, reliably live): ^VIX÷^VIX3M term structure, QQQ/SPY 20-day dollar-volume ratio, SOXX relative volume.

      Percentiles & crowd contributions — every indicator is framed as a 3-year percentile: COT gets a true percentile from fetched history on live refresh (tagged live_3y); the rest are linearly interpolated on curated KB anchor bands (tagged curated_band — estimates). Crowd contribution = the percentile, inverted (100 − pct) for indicators where LOW means crowded (put/call, VIX term structure).

      Composite crowdedness (0 = uncrowded, 100 = one-sided) — hard-coded, disclosed weights: COT 20%, margin-debt YoY 20%, NAAIM 15%, put/call 15%, AAII 10%, VIX term 10%, QQQ/SPY volume 5%, SOXX volume 5%. Verdict thresholds: <40 UNCROWDED, 40–75 CROWDED, ≥75 ONE-SIDED. The nuance read uses 3 crack tells — the /credit card's CCC−IG decompression flag (aligned with /credit 2026-07-02, ON), VIX÷VIX3M ≥0.95, and put/call ≥0.70 & rising; ≥2 tells on = "crowded & cracking", otherwise the build trend (majority of COT/NAAIM/margin 4-week changes positive) splits "crowded & rising" from "crowded & stalling".

      Assumptions & limits — COT covers futures only; AAII is a small survey; margin debt lags 2 months; 13F (quarterly + 45d) is declared out of the free tier; percentiles on curated bands are estimates; volume proxies measure attention, not positions; 0DTE flow has no free structured source. Positioning is NOT a demand signal — it sets the violence of the drawdown, not the fundamentals. /aibubble (market 64.6 overheated, 2026-07-02) is cited as context only, not scored. Manual password-gated refresh only, no schedule. AI synthesis is an interpretive aid — not investment advice.
